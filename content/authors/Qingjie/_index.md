Qingjie Meng is an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science at University of Birmingham, where she leads the Medical AI Lab. She is an Honorary Research Fellow at Imperial College London. Before joining Birmingham, she was a Research Associate in the Biomedical Image Analysis Group (BioMedIA), Department of Computing at Imperial College London, working with Prof. Daniel Rueckert and Prof. Declan O'Regan. She obtained her Ph.D. in computer vision for medical imaging from the Department of Computing at Imperial College London, supervised by Dr. Bernhard Kainz and Prof. Daniel Rueckert. Before that, she obtained her M.S. and B.S. from Northwestern Polytechnical University, supervised by Prof. Yanning Zhang. She joined RISE-MICCAI as Workshop Coordinator and is a Board Member of RISE-MICCAI and Women in MICCAI. Her research interests include medical imaging analysis, computer vision, and machine learning, with focus on image and video generation, 3D shape reconstruction, pathological image analysis, explainable AI, and learning with low supervision.
